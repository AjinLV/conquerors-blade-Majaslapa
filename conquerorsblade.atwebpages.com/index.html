>Fief Quest Bulletpoints<
  • *There is a limit of 20 Fief quests a week

  • *Don't do all your Fief Quests

  • *You gain prestige and xp from daily quests, completing them all early restricts your next daily quest completion

  • *Don't go for the highest star quests

  • *Some high star quests give less than lower star

  • *Highly recommended to do Fief quests in Green/Blue territory

  • *To see the colour, select "Diplomacy" bottom right

  • *These quests grant the "Other" bonus upon completion

  • *You can gain resources even if your wagon is full, they are stored in an "Resource Pile"

  • *Trough Requisition Orders, it costs less bronze to "Gather"

  • *For highest efficiency of time and resoruces, It's recommended to build 5 Serf units, and level them to 7

  • *It's simple to accomplish in Bandit Raid or Expeditions

  • *By going to gather with 5 serfs, you get the most resources

  • *Resources reset when they are "low" or at 1PM CEST
